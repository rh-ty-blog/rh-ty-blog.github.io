# Day 1
On day 1 I arrived at 9 and I was introduced to our supervisers for the week and got to know the rest of the people in my group. Then we got a tour of the Red Hat facility. After that we played Mario kart and pool, the games were very competitive and fun. Shortly after the games we got a 1 hour lunch break which was very relaxing. After the lunch break I learned about Agile and we were then put into groups and did a simulation of shareholders in agile where they told us how to design our potato heads and in groups we worked together to build our product. My groups product was a star wars themed potato head and I think it turned out quite good. Overall day 1 was very enjoyable and I learned a lot in the Agile workshop. I also enjoyed getting to know loads of new people.
